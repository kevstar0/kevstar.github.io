Frequently asked questions


I have connectivity issues

Reboot your router, or contact contact us for assitance. If no reponce is given within 1 hr please call 136 if you're an orange user and drop your query and we shall assist you momentarily


I want to subscribe/ renew my service

Please visit Mobile fly and select your package, you will be charged for the chosen service via Ewallet services and connectivity will resume shortly.


How many devices can i connect?

A minimum of 2 devices is recommended and a max of 5 for home usage


How much data will i need?

The data you need depends on the number of devices you have

Due to new tech developing data has to be shared amongst TV's, Bluetooth, Wifi routers, phones, laptops and other tech available

  • 1 device (phone or laptop) = 12Gb
  • 2 (phone & laptop) = 12Gb
  • 3 devices (with a variation of 2 phones and 1 laptop or computer) = 36Gb
  • 4 < 5 = unlimited

    • But that depends on the number of devices you may want to connect to

    Tv's, computers and laptops may use more bandwith (data) and may cap your connection short as we try to divide the same connection to other users
